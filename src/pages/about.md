# About Khalid Naami

Welcome to the digital library and research hub of **Khalid Naami**. 

I am an academic writer, quantitative analyst, and investigative researcher. As the **Founder & CEO at Dashboard Options**, my work bridges the gap between complex market microstructure, options theory, and macroeconomic geopolitical forces.

---

## My Mission

My goal is to provide institutional-grade financial intelligence and rigorous geopolitical analysis. Modern markets do not move on news stories or basic charts; they are driven by the mechanical hedging flows of market makers and the deep undercurrents of global resource security. This website serves as a repository for my research, tools, and publications.

---

## Areas of Expertise

### 📈 Quantitative Finance & Option Greeks
At Dashboard Options, we analyze the structural forces of option flow. By tracking:
*   **Gamma Exposure (GEX)**: Understanding how dealer hedging anchors or accelerates market volatility.
*   **Vanna & Charm**: Modeling how changing implied volatility and time decay alter market dealer delta positions.
*   **Implied Volatility (IV) Surfaces**: Extracting real market expectations from option pricing anomalies.

### 🌍 Global Macro & Geopolitics
I study the geopolitical risk factors that dictate capital flows:
*   **Energy & Resource Security**: Modeling conflicts, maritime blockades, and logistics choke points (e.g., Strait of Hormuz, Bab-el-Mandeb).
*   **Military Technology**: Investigating electronic warfare capabilities, hypersonic ballistic systems, and satellite technology shifts.
*   **Monetary & Credit Cycles**: Monitoring central bank balance sheets, private credit expansion, and sovereign debt risks.
