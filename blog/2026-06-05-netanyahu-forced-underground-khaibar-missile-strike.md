# Netanyahu Forced Underground: Khaibar Strike Misses

Israeli Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu has transitioned his administration entirely to classified underground bunkers following a high-precision missile strike targeting his office complex. The attack, which utilized Iran's newly deployed **Khorramshahr-4 (Khaibar Shekan)** ballistic missile, reportedly missed the Prime Minister's location by a narrow margin of just nine minutes and four seconds. 

The Quds Force of the Islamic Revolutionary Guard Corps (IRGC) has officially placed both Netanyahu and the commander of the Israeli Air Force (IAF) at the top of their target agenda, prompting unprecedented security protocols to secure the Israeli leadership.

## The Khorramshahr-4 Strike on Tel Aviv

The strike targeted the primary ministerial offices in Tel Aviv. The weapon used, the Khorramshahr-4 (or Khaibar Shekan), represents the latest evolution of Iran's heavy liquid-propellant ballistic missile family, featuring several advanced technical capabilities:
*   **Massive Payload:** The missile carries a 1,500-kilogram high-explosive, armor-piercing warhead, making it one of the heaviest payloads in the region.
*   **Terminal Speed:** The missile achieves velocities of Mach 16 outside the atmosphere and Mach 8 (approximately 9,800 km/h) inside the atmosphere during its terminal phase.
*   **Maneuverability without Wings:** The warhead cone does not contain external fins or wings; instead, it uses terminal thrust-vectoring steering. This reduces atmospheric drag, increasing both speed and accuracy while allowing it to bypass THAAD and Arrow interceptors.
*   **Reaction Time:** The missile has a preparation time of under 15 minutes and can be stored in underground silos fully fueled for years.

In addition to the missile strikes, Netanyahu's personal residence was recently penetrated by a Hezbollah loitering munition that reached his bedroom window, confirming that the prime minister is directly targeted by regional forces.

## Extreme Security Protocols and Media Silence

To protect the Prime Minister's location from advanced acoustic analysis and satellite tracking, his security detail has implemented extreme communication restrictions. These measures have caused substantial domestic criticism, with media outlets like *Ma'ariv* and journalists Yair Kosin reporting on the growing lack of official information:
1.  **Fifty-Second Video Limit:** All public statements released by Netanyahu's office are strictly capped at 50 seconds to prevent Iranian electronic intelligence from using voice-signature tracking and AI-driven geolocation algorithms to pinpoint his active bunker.
2.  **Spokesperson Blackout:** The Prime Minister's communications team, led by Zeev Feldman, has suspended regular briefings and is refusing to respond to press queries.
3.  **Constant Relocation:** Netanyahu is being moved continuously between various underground command nodes, preventing the establishment of a fixed office footprint.

In addition to targeting the Prime Minister, the Quds Force has designated the commander of the Israeli Air Force (IAF) as a primary target. Because the IAF serves as the main offensive tool for strikes inside Iran, its leadership is viewed by Tehran as directly responsible for the regional escalation.

## Regional Air Defense System Failures

The direct threat to Netanyahu comes as Israel's regional allies struggle with defensive system failures. The recent [cyber-attack that grounded U.S. F-15s in Kuwait](/blog/us-f15-cyber-attack-kuwait-crashes) and the [destruction of the UAE THAAD radar in Ruwais](/blog/thaad-radar-destroyed-uae-air-defense-pierced) have degraded the coalition's air defense capabilities. 

As the [Trump administration seeks direct talks with the new Iranian council](/blog/trump-agrees-to-talk-with-irans-new-leaders) to secure a ceasefire, Netanyahu's team is under pressure to accept a diplomatic solution to avoid a catastrophic security breach in Tel Aviv.
